Autumn Destinations

Places to Visit in Autumn

Ladak

Ladakh, located in northern India, is a high-altitude desert known for its stunning landscapes, including the Pangong Lake and Nubra Valley. It features majestic mountains, Buddhist monasteries, and a unique culture blending Tibetan and Indian influences, attracting adventure enthusiasts and spiritual seekers.

Spiti Valley

Spiti Valley, in Himachal Pradesh, India, is renowned for its stark landscapes, ancient monasteries, vibrant Tibetan culture, and adventure trekking opportunities.

Pondicherry

Pondicherry, a coastal city in India, is famous for its French colonial architecture, serene beaches, vibrant cafes, and spiritual Auroville community.
