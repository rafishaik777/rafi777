Power Star Pawan Kalyan

Biography

Power Star Pawan Kalyan, born Konidela Kalyan Babu, is an Indian actor, producer, and politician.

He began his career in films as a child actor with the film 'Akira' in 1991 and later made his debut as a lead actor with 'Akkada Ammayi Ikkada Abbayi' in 1996.

Achievements

  • Recipient of several awards including Filmfare Awards and Nandi Awards
  • Founder of the Jana Sena Party, a political party in Andhra Pradesh
  • Known for his philanthropic work and social initiatives

Political Background

Pawan Kalyan entered politics in 2014 and founded the Jana Sena Party with the aim of bringing about political accountability and social change.

He has been actively involved in various social and political movements and has garnered a significant following among the youth.
