Biography

Pawan Kalyan, born as Konidela Kalyan Babu, is an Indian actor, politician, and film producer. He made his acting debut in 1996 with the film "Akkada Ammayi Ikkada Abbayi". Over the years, he has gained immense popularity and a massive following among fans for his versatile acting, unique style, and powerful performances. Some of his most notable works include "Gabbar Singh", "Attarintiki Daredi", "Jalsa", "Tholi Prema", and "Kushi". Besides his film career, Pawan Kalyan is also actively involved in politics and social work, making significant contributions to society through his philanthropic activities.

Achievements

  • Multiple Filmfare Awards South winner
  • Founder of Jana Sena Party
  • Listed in Forbes India Celebrity 100
  • Recognized for his philanthropic activities
  • Recipient of the SIIMA Award for Best Actor
  • Honored with the Nandi Award for Best Actor

About Jana Sena Party

The Jana Sena Party is an Indian political party founded by Pawan Kalyan in March 2014. The party's objective is to address various issues faced by the people of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana, focusing on transparency, accountability, and social justice. The party has a strong commitment to fighting corruption, improving governance, and ensuring the welfare of the common people. Under Pawan Kalyan's leadership, the Jana Sena Party aims to bring a positive change in society by advocating for the rights of the underprivileged and working towards sustainable development.
